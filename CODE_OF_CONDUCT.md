# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for
all, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Be constructive and professional.
- Assume good intent and help others learn.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruptions.

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project
maintainers.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and events.
